# Zerus & Ona

Zerus & Ona explores technology, values, and identity through stories for children and families.
The project has evolved through several phases, from early published books to an ongoing series of fables designed to be read and discussed together.

- [Early published books](https://www.zerusxona.com/books)
- [Ongoing series of fables (in Spanish)](https://zerusxona.substack.com/)
